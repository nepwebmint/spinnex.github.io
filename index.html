Frequently Asked Questions

How does JpgBolt convert images?

JpgBolt uses client-side JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas to process your images directly in your browser. This means your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and fast conversion speeds.

What image formats are supported?

JpgBolt supports conversion from PNG, WEBP, PDF, JPEG, GIF formats to JPG. We also support image compression and resizing for most common formats.

Is there a file size limit?

Since processing happens in your browser, very large images might slow down the conversion process. For optimal performance, we recommend images under 10MB.

Are my images stored anywhere?

No, your images are processed entirely in your browser and never uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security for your files.

Is JpgBolt completely free to use?

Yes, JpgBolt is completely free with no hidden costs or limitations. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone.

What happens with animated GIFs?

When converting animated GIFs to JPG, only the first frame of the animation will be converted to a static JPG image.

How does image compression work?

Our image compressor reduces file size by optimizing the image quality while maintaining visual integrity. You can adjust the compression level to balance between file size and quality.

Can I resize images without losing quality?

Our resizer maintains image quality by using smart resizing algorithms. You can resize images while preserving aspect ratio or set custom dimensions.
